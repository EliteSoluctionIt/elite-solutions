About Elite Solutions

We know the area, the vendors, the seasonality and the expectations of those who delegate the care of an important home.

Why three levels

Each property and each family has different needs. That is why we built our services into three progressive bundles:

  • Guardian · property control and protection.
  • Steward · day-to-day management of works.
  • Director · strategic oversight and direction.

Each level always includes the previous one: moving up to Steward does not mean losing Guardian’s checks; with Director you get the full package.
